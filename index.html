Hi, I’m Alexandr Kalinin

I’m an International Postdoctoral Fellow jointly at the Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data (SRIBD) and the University of Michigan, working with Xiang Wan and Brian Athey. My research focuses on biomedical image analysis, machine learning, and visual analytics.

Prior to that, I received my PhD in Bioinformatics from the Department of Computational Medicine and Bioinformatics at the University of Michigan, where I worked with Ivo Dinov and Brian Athey.
