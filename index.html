Biography

Alexandr Kalinin is a PhD Candidate in Bioinformatics at the University of Michigan. His research interests include understanding of high-dimensional 3D microscope images of cells and their compartments using machine and deep learning, signal processing, high performance computing, etc. He also works on designing and developing web-based tools for interactive data analysis and visualization. He is co-supervised by Dr. Ivo Dinov and Dr. Brian Athey.
